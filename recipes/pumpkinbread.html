Ingredients

  • pumpkinbread mix
  • 6 tablespoon butter
  • two eggs

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Mix ingreidents in bowl until you reach a smooth consistency
  3. Pour evenly into a 4x6x4 baking pan
  4. Bake for 50 minutes or until a wooden toothpick inserted inside the loaf can be pulled out dry
