the Lasagna

Description

I've been reading books of old, the legends and the myths Achilles and his gold, Hercules and his gifts
Spider-Man's control and Batman with his fists. And clearly, I don't see myself upon that list... LASAGNA!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • Lasagna noodles (fresh or dried)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Recipe

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Olive oil down:
    • heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté onions and garlic until they’re golden, not burnt!
    • add the beef and brown it. For heaven's sake, don’t overcrowd the pan!
    • stir in tomatoes, purée, salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 30 minutes. Taste it! If it’s bland, add more seasoning!
  3. Prepare the béchamel:
    • melt the butter in a saucepan. Whisk in the flour to form a roux. No lumps allowed!
    • gradually add warm milk, whisking until smooth. Cook until thickened. Season properly—don’t skimp!
  4. Assemble the lasagna:
    • in a baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagna sheets, béchamel, and mozzarella. Repeat! Finish with béchamel and a glorious layer of mozzarella. Make it look good!
  5. Bake:
    • preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake for 30-40 minutes until it’s golden and bubbling like it should be. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving—don’t dive in like a barbarian!
