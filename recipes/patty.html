Ingredients

  • frozen patty
  • block of cheese
  • toaster
  • microwave

Recipe

  1. Gather patty and cheese. For this recipe, we'll be using a frozen patty
  2. Olive oil down!
  3. Set microwave to ~2:00 (varies by microwave wattage)
  4. Tear small hole in patty packaging as indicated per instructions
  5. Heat while simultaniously grating CHEDDAR cheese
  6. Open patty, covering meat with cheese. Toast until desired toastiness achieved
